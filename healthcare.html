Healthcare vs Counterfeits

One of the leading healthcare device manufacturer has identified a surge in counterfeit medical products—including falsely marketed monitors and therapeutic devices. These items that are not part of the genuine product line, mimic the  authentic branding and packaging, failing to meet the required quality or safety standards.

Health Risks & Consumer Urgency

Counterfeit medical devices can deliver life-impacting consequences—misleading readings can cause delayed treatment, misdiagnosis, or failure to detect health issues.

Brands Response to counterfeiting

Measures applied to ensure consumer safety:

  • Issue public advisories against unauthorized online purchases.
  • Provide visual guides to assist in identifying counterfeit devices.
  • Initiate legal actions and enforce intellectual property rights against violators.
  • Emphasize on a safety-first policy by disclaiming liability for counterfeit-related damages.
  • Encourage product authentication through official verification channels.

How ChainShields Takes Protection to the Next Level

ChainShields offers a proactive and continuous defense, creating a transparent, tamper-proof link from factory to consumer:

  • Manufacturer-Regulated Supply Chain
    ChainShields embeds blockchain-tracked QR tags on every device and accessory at the point of manufacturing. Every subsequent transfer from distributors, warehouses to retailers is recorded and verifiable, eliminating unauthorized insertion of fake products into the supply chain.
  • Consumer-Level Transparency
    End users can scan the ChainShields QR code to access a secure, detailed journey of the product—where and when it moved, who handled it, and whether any deviations occurred. No more guessing if your wrist monitor or nebulizer is genuine.
  • Instant Alerts & Risk Detection
    Any skipped step, unknown handler, or mismatched location in the scan audit instantly triggers alerts. Both the provider and the end user receive immediate notification of possible fraud, allowing swift investigation and response.
  • Immutable & Auditable Records
    Built on blockchain, ChainShields records supply chain data irrevocably, giving full visibility and peace of mind to both provider and its customers.
